British Bangers recipe

British Bangers are the essential sausage for a true English fry-up. This tried and tested recipe combines tips from many family members who all claim to make the best sausages, culminating in the ultimate British Banger.

Ingredients

  • 500g pork shoulder
  • 50g pork fat
  • 100g breadcrumbs
  • Handful of sage
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Mix your ingredients in a bowl
  2. Use the sausage stuffer attachment to carefully pump the mixture into your casing before twisting into 6 inch sausages
  3. Cook under the grill
